Egg Fried Rice Recipe

Description

Egg Fried Rice Recipe, simple rice dish is made with eggs, cooked rice, soy sauce and vinegar. Fried rice is a great quick lunch option which is easy to make and taste so delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup uncooked instant rice
  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • 1/2 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 large egg, lightly beaten
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Bring water, soy sauce, and salt to a boil in a medium saucepan. Stir in instant rice and remove from heat. Cover and let stand for 5 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a medium skillet or wok over medium heat. Saute onions and green beans in hot oil for 2 to 3 minutes. Pour in beaten egg and fry for 2 minutes, scrambling egg while it cooks.
  3. Add cooked rice to egg mixture; mix well. Season with pepper.
