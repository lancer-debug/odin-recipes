French Fries

Description

There's nothing quite like the enticingly crisp skin and fluffy interior of a french fry. You can make this snack at home with just 3 ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 large russet potato, cut into evenly sized strips
  • Vegetable oil for frying, about 2 cups or as needed
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Soak potato strips in a large bowl of water for about 30 minutes.
  2. Pat with paper towels until thoroughly dry.
  3. Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 275 degrees F. Gently add potatoes to the hot oil and fry for about 5 minutes, stirring and flipping the potatoes occasionally.
  4. Use a slotted spoon to transfer to a paper towel-lined plate. Let cool completely.
  5. Heat oil again, but this time to 350 degrees F. Add potatoes and fry a second time until golden brown, 5 to 6 minutes.
  6. Remove from the deep-fryerand blot with a paper towel. Sprinkle with salt to serve.
